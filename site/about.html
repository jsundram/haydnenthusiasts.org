ABOUT

The Haydn Enthusiasts were founded in May, 2013 by Andrew Wong, Elaine Leisinger, Jason Sundram, and Lewis Patzner.

We were brought together by a shared enjoyment of Haydn's String Quartets, and formed The Enthusiasts out of a desire to explore all 68.5 of them, and share our journey and enthusiasm with as many people as we can along the way.

The Haydn Enthusiasts perform monthly at Vessel Gallery and Revolution Cafe, and can occasionally be spotted at Glen Park Station and Awaken Cafe. Follow us on facebook and twitter to see what we're up to.
